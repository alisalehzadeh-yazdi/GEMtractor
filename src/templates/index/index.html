welcome, enalyzer!

This is the enalyzer. The Enzyme Network anALYZER. :)

It supports you in analyzing enzyme networks.

Give it an SBML model and it will help you to filter currency metabolites, to simulate knock out experiments, or to reduce the model size to e.g. focus on your flux balance results. And much more...

There is an API, so you may use the enalyzer programmatically. The analyzer is free software and also available as a Docker image.
