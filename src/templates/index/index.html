This is the GEMtractor. It supports you in analyzing genome-scale metabolic models (GEMs)!

Choose an SBML model and it will help you to filter currency metabolites, to simulate knock out experiments, or to reduce the model size to e.g. focus on your flux balance results. And much more...

You may then extract the protein-interaction-network from the concentrated model, and finally export you network in several formats for subsequent analyses.

There is an API, so you may use the enalyzer from your programs. The analyzer is free software and also available as a Docker image. Thus, running your own analyzer is as easy as calling a single command line — including all the benefits, such as privacy and speed! Read more about the setup at ... TODO
